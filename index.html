Who We Are

The Aggie Club of Engineers is a social, service, and leadership organization strongly rooted in academics. ACE is founded on the idea that engineers should not only develop strongly in academics but also be of strong character. Our aim is to develop our members into successful Aggies and leaders while improving Aggieland as a whole by giving back to the community.
